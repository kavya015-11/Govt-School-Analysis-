Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) & Tamil Nadu Government Schools

1. No Poverty: Government schools provide free education, mid-day meals, and scholarships, helping underprivileged students escape poverty.

2. Zero Hunger: The mid-day meal scheme ensures children receive nutritious food, reducing hunger and improving academic performance.

3. Good Health and Well-being: Schools focus on student health through medical checkups, hygiene education, and sports programs.

4. Quality Education: Tamil Nadu government schools offer free education, digital learning tools, and skill-based training to ensure quality education for all.
