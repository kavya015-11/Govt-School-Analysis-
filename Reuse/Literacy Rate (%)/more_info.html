📊 Literacy Analysis Across Indian Regions

🌏 North India

  • 📖 Delhi and Punjab have high literacy rates due to strong urban infrastructure.
  • 📘 Government schemes in Haryana and Uttar Pradesh aim to bridge rural-urban disparities.

🌄 South India

  • 🏆 Kerala leads with near-total literacy due to community-driven initiatives.
  • 💻 Tamil Nadu and Karnataka focus on digital literacy and higher education access.

📚 East India

  • 📜 West Bengal emphasizes girl child education through schemes like Kanyashree.
  • 🏫 Odisha and Bihar are working on improving school enrollment and teacher training.

🌍 West India

  • 🌟 Maharashtra has extensive literacy programs for both urban and rural populations.
  • 📘 Gujarat’s school infrastructure development has improved educational accessibility.
